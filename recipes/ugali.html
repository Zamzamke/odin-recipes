Ugali Recipe

Ugali is a common food in Kenya.It is made from maize flour mixed with boiling water under high heat and pound until it becomes stiff.It is normally served with a stew on the side.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Corn flour
  • 2 cups water

Instructions

  1. In a pan bring the water to boil.
  2. Add the one cup corn flour into the water and begin to stir using a wooden spoon.Keep adding a handful of flour as you mix until you achieve a thick consistency.
  3. Reduce the heat to medium and let it sit for five minutes.Keep turning the ugaki as it continues to stick together.
  4. Once it has become firm,gather it into a round ball and turn it over into a plate.
  5. Slice it intopieces and serve it with a stew or vegetable of choice.
