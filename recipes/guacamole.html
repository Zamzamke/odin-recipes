Guacamole Recipe

This is an easy guacamole recipe that can be made within five minutes.It can be served as a side or as a topping

Ingredients

  • 2 avocados
  • 1 onion
  • 2 tomatoes
  • 2 limes
  • cilantro
  • salt

Steps

  • Chop the onions and tomatoes and cilnatro into fine pieces and mix them.
  • Mash the avocado in a bowl and add the mixture of the tomatoes and onions.
  • Mix it well and squeeze in the lime juice into the bowl to add taste and to keep the avocado fresh.
  • Cover it and Refrigirate for a 15 minutes then serve it as you wish
