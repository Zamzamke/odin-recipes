Mango Pickles

Pickled mangoes are a delicacy and easy to make for a snack.It has a perfect balance of tangy and sweet and are an extra boost for vitamin C and fiber.

Ingredients

  • 5 raw mangoes
  • 2 tablespoons salt
  • 2 cups boiling water
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 tablespoon vinegar
  • A mason jar

Steps

  • Slice the mangoes into thick slices and remove the skin.Add two tablespoon salt and let it sit in a bowl for 15minutes
  • In a jug,add the boiling water,vinegar and sugar then stir it until the sugar dissolves.
  • Pour the solvent into the mango and let it sit till it cools down.Once it has cooled down transfer it into the mason jar and seal it.Place it in the refrigirator for about two days till it turns yellow.
